[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
[Warm acoustic strumming – inviting and intimate]
I see the moonlight resting on your skin tonight
We got nowhere to be and no reason to fight
Come a little closer, let the shadows fall
I want to be the one who answers when you call

[Verse 1]
[Soft fingerpicked strings enter]
There is a quiet fire burning in the hearth
Tracing every line that travels down your path
You pull me in with just a single glance
Two lovers swaying in a sensual dance

[Pre-Chorus]
[Light piano accents begin to build]
The evening breeze is whispering our names
Caught inside the beauty of these gentle flames
I feel the gravity of every breath you take
This is a memory I never want to break

[Chorus]
[Smooth melodies with gentle acoustic percussion]
Wrap me up inside your warm embrace
I find my heaven in the contours of your face
We are slowing down the hands of every clock
Lost in the rhythm of this midnight walk

[Post-Chorus]
[Subtle layered voices]
[Background vocal] (Wrapped inside your gentle charm)
Just a tender touch to keep me safe from harm

[Verse 2]
[Subtle organic rhythm joins the warm tone]
Your eyes are holding secrets I could spend a life reading
A silent conversation that my body is slowly leading
You pour a little honey into everything you say
Chasing all the shadows of the world away

[Pre-Chorus 2]
[Low strings deepen slightly, harmonies layer up]
The evening breeze is whispering our names
Caught inside the beauty of these gentle flames
I feel the gravity of every breath you take
This is a memory I never want to break

[Chorus]
[Emotional spacing, smooth melodic lift]
Wrap me up inside your warm embrace
I find my heaven in the contours of your face
We are slowing down the hands of every clock
Lost in the rhythm of this midnight walk

[Post-Chorus 2]
[Acoustic emotional texture shines through]
[Background vocal] (Wrapped inside your gentle charm)
Just a tender touch to keep me safe from harm

[Bridge]
[Stripped down to light piano, intimate voice]
We leave the heavy burdens far behind
A sweet surrender of a peaceful mind
And every word Saeed will ever write
Is pouring out into the open night
I pull you closer to my steady heart
We swore that we would never be apart

[Spoken]
[Breathy emotional delivery, cozy late-night feel]
[Spoken] (Just lay your head right here on my chest.)
[Spoken] (This is exactly where we belong.)

[Final Chorus]
[Soulful dynamic phrasing, biggest emotional lift]
